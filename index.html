About Late Night Miami

Late Night Miami was created in the fall of 2015 to provide students with entertainment every Thursday, Friday, and Saturday night. From coffee houses to craft nights, these weekend events are great for study breaks, entertaining visitors, or simply meeting someone new! Late Night Miami helps fund student organizations' events when they cannot provide the funding and resources themselves.
